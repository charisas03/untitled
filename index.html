  • What is Computer Science?

    Simply put, it is the study of the principals and use of computers. Computer science can be found everywhere! It is used in your phone, any website (like this one!), your TV, and even the stoplights at an intersection. Pretty much every electronic device was made through computer science, and in our technologically advancing world, this study is becoming increasingly important! It has been instrumental in increasing efficiency and our ability to solve complex computational problems quickly. Believe it or not, computer science has been around since as early as 1623, and has grown exponentially since then. Frankly, computers can work faster than us humans can, but in order for them to solve our problems, they need instructions. That's where computer scientists come into play.
